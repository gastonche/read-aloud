The History of Tea

Tea is an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot water over cured leaves. After water, it is the most widely consumed drink in the world.

According to legend, tea was first discovered in ancient China. A leaf is said to have drifted into the emperor's boiling water. He found the resulting infusion refreshing, and a tradition was born.

Today tea is grown across many regions. Its preparation varies widely between cultures, from delicate ceremonies to everyday convenience.
